Biography

Ustaz Kazim Elias, full name Mohammad Kazim bin Elias, is a well-known Malaysian Islamic preacher, speaker, and educator. He is widely recognized for his engaging lectures and deep knowledge of Islamic teachings, which he shares through various media platforms, including television, radio, and social media.

Born on November 28, 1972, in Kampung Paya, Perak, Ustaz Kazim Elias has devoted his life to spreading Islamic knowledge and promoting moral values. He received his early education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Kampung Paya and later pursued his Islamic studies at several renowned institutions, including Maahad Tahfiz Al-Quran Wal Qiraat in Kuala Kangsar and Maahad Tahfiz Al-Quran in Kelantan.
