Ustaz Kazim's unique approach to preaching, which combines humor with insightful religious teachings, has garnered him a large following. He is the founder and principal of Al-Barakah Education Centre in Manjoi, Perak, where he continues to teach and inspire the younger generation. His efforts in promoting Islamic education and values have made him a respected figure in the Malaysian Muslim community.
