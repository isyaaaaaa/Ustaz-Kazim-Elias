Politics

Ustaz Kazim Elias has occasionally been linked to politics in Malaysia. He has been known to share his views on political issues and has sometimes been associated with political figures. However, his primary focus remains on his role as a preacher and motivational speaker. While he has influence in the socio-political sphere due to his prominence as a religious figure, he is not formally involved in politics as a politician or a member of a political party.
